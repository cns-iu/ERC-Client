Interface Operations

Operation Description

Left Click on a Node
  • If not clicked already - Selects the node, highlights its connections, opens the publication popup and the corresponding entries in bar graph.
  • If already clicked - Clears the Selection.

Left Click on a Bar-Graph Entry
  • If not clicked already - Selects the bar graph entry, highlights corresponding node in network, its connections and their corresponding bar graph entries, opens its publication popup and pans the map to its location.
  • If clicked already - Clears the Selection.

Mouseover on a Node 		Highlights the node, its connections and corresponding bar-graph entries.

Mouseover on a Bar-Graph Entry 		Highlights the bar-graph entry, its corresponding node in the network and its connections and their corresponding bar graph entries.

Mouse-Scroll 		Zoom In/Out

Double Click on Screen 		Zoom in
