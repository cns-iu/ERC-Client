Co-authorship Geospatial Visualization

This interactive visualization shows the co-authorship network overlaid on a geospatial map of the world.

  • Each node represents an author.
  • Two authors are connected if they have authored a paper together.
  • Circle area sizes encode the number of links per node, also called node degree.
  • Horizontal bar-graph aids in better understanding of data.
  • The legend details how the information being encoded.
