Interface Operations

Operations Action

Left Click on a Node 		If not clicked already: Selects the node, highlights its connections, highlights their corresponding entries in the bar graph and opens the publication popup.
If already clicked: Clears the selection.

Left Click on a Bar Graph entry 		If not clicked already: Selects the bar graph entry, highlights corresponding node in network, its connections and their corresponding bar graph entries, opens the node's publication popup and pans the map to its location.
If clicked already: Clears the selection.

Mouseover on a Node 		Highlights the node, its connections and their corresponding bar graph entries.

Mouseover on a Bar Graph entry 		Highlights the bar graph entry, its corresponding node and connections in the network and their corresponding bar graph entries.

Mouse-Scroll 		Zoom in/out.

Double Click on screen 		Zoom in.
