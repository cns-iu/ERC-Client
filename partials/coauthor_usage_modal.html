Interface Operations

Operation Action

Left Click on a Node 		Selects the node, highlights its connections, opens the publication popup and the corresponding entry in bar graph.

Left Click on a Bar Graph entry 		Selects the bar graph entry, corresponding node in network, its publication popup and its connections.

Mouseover on a Node 		Highlights the node, its connections and corresponding bar graph entry.

Mouseover on an Edge 		Highlights the edge and connected nodes, their labels and their corresponding bar-graph entries.

Mouseover on a Bar Graph Entry 		Highlights the bar graph entry, its corresponding node in the network and its connections.

Right Click on a Node 		Produces a color-picker button for color selection of the node.

Left Click on Slider 		Filters node labels.

Mouse-Scroll 		Zoom in/out.

Left Click on screen 		Clears the color-picker and/or clears any node/bar-graph entry selection.

Double Click on screen 		Zoom in.
