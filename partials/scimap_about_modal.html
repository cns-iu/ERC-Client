Interface Operations

Operation Description

Left Click on a Node 		Opens the publication popup.

Mouseover on a Node 		Displays labels of all nodes from the same discipline.

Mouseover on an Edge 		Highlights the edge and connected nodes and their labels.

Mouse-Scroll 		Zoom In/Out on the nodes.

Left Click on Screen 		Hides publication pop-up.

Double Click on Screen 		Zoom in on the nodes.

Left Click on B/W button 		Toggle Screen Background Color - Black/White.
