Science Map Visualization

This interactive map supports the exploration of expertise profiles for a researcher, center, department, or university overlaid on a science map so that outliers, clusters, and trends can be discerned.

  • 554 subdisciplines of science are shown, aggregated into 13 color-coded, labelled disciplines.
  • Each circle denotes a subdiscipline.
  • Circle area sizes are proportional to the number of papers published per subdiscipline.
  • The legend details how the information is being encoded.
